Jhon was an exceptionally great friend, and a great husband, his life was sadly cut short from an angry substitute teacher who threw him in the trash…

Jhon grew up in a teachers Tupperware box with his close family, his wife Jill Burgur, and his father, Jeremy Burgur. His other relatives live in a storage facility. Jhon liked to fly kites with his father, his favorite kite was the green one. Jhon disliked substitute teachers or being thrown around, making his saddening death more emotional. Despite his living conditions and not having many friends, he was always able to stay positive. His favorite snack to share with his family was hummus (just the sauce). Jhon was an exceptional finger wrestler and pole vaulting, winning five Olympic metals. Jhon was known to only wear suits, track outfits, or people who live in Yatutsk, gaining a bit of popularity for his unique clothing style. His favorite game was Table Leg Repair Simulator, he was so interested he bought the Chair Leg Repairs DLC, he never really played right, he just guessed. Another game he was interested in was California Raisins the Grape Escape, with more that 400 hours on the game. His favorite place to regularly visit was Surtsey. Jhon’s last wish was to repair a table leg in real life, he tried is best but could never get it.
